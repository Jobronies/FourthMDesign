Insight

We are a group of high school students who screen print and heat press vinyl onto an array of clothing; mostly shirts and hoodies, but upon request, we do almost any type of clothing. We do special orders and make custom shirts, in bulk, or singles. For example, in the past we have done shirts for school programs, sports teams, and local bands. On top of custom shirts, we also sell our own self designed apparel that represent landmarks and features of the Northwest.

Origins

Fourth Moon Design was founded in early 2016 as a school project in the city of Sherwood, Oregon. Fourth Moon struggled with start up costs, and the learning curve of pressing and screen printing shirts, with a consistent standard of quality. We feature designs such as Mount Hood and the Portland Skyline to reflect our Northwest origins.
